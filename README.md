> **Empowering Communities. Restoring Ecosystems. Building Wealth Through Conservation.**

ONE-JAZ is a blockchain-powered platform that transforms Kenya's ambitious JazaMiti tree-planting initiative into a transparent, rewarding, and economically sustainable ecosystem. By leveraging OneChain's high-performance blockchain, gamification mechanics, and verified carbon credits, we're creating a model where environmental action generates measurable economic value for communities.

Kenya aims to plant **15 billion trees by 2032** to combat climate change, restore biodiversity, and rehabilitate degraded ecosystems. ONE-JAZ accelerates this mission by:

- **Verifying** tree plantings with multi-layer AI, satellite imagery, and community validation
- **Rewarding** participants immediately with tokens (OJZ) and achievement NFTs
- **Monetizing** conservation through carbon credits and corporate partnerships
- **Empowering** communities with transparent governance and economic ownership

## ðŸ“± User Flows

### For Tree Planters

1. **Register**: Create account and link OneWallet
2. **Plant**: Plant trees following best practices
3. **Submit**: Capture GPS coordinates + photo evidence via app
4. **Verify**: Wait 24-48 hours for multi-layer verification
5. **Earn**: Receive OJZ tokens and track toward NFT milestones
6. **Stake**: Lock tokens in pools for yield (optional)
7. **Trade**: Buy/sell NFTs on marketplace

### For Community Organizations

1. **Enroll**: Register CFA with organizational profile
2. **Campaign**: Create tree-planting campaigns with targets
3. **Mobilize**: Recruit participants and distribute seedlings
4. **Monitor**: Track submissions via admin dashboard
5. **Earn**: Receive 5% bonus OJZ for campaign completion
6. **Govern**: Vote on platform decisions via DAO

### For Corporate Partners

1. **Partner**: Sign sustainability agreement (Bronze - Platinum)
2. **Fund**: Contribute capital to reward pools
3. **Engage**: Provide employee access to planting programs
4. **Report**: Receive verified carbon offset certificates
5. **Brand**: Showcase environmental impact in ESG reports
